Locust pose












Instructions


  1. Begin with lying down on tummy with face down in Makarasana (Crocodile Pose). Here relax the spine with few breaths and connect the breathing with the movement of the spine.
  2. With feet close to each other and chin on the floor, place your hands /arms close to your body to the side, palms facing up.
  3. While you take a deep breathe, raise both legs from the waist , keeping the legs together and knees straight at about 40 degrees from the floor or as per .
  4. With every inhalation raise the legs higher by supporting the upper thighs with your palms and straighten the legs behind you as much as possible.

Benefits


  1. Helps with lower back pain
  2. Helps with hamstring injuries
  3. Increases arm strength
  4. Improves general weakness
  5. Elevates mood
  6. Improves posture problems that come from spending too much time hunched over
  7. Helps with arthritis of the knees
